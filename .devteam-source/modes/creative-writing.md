# Mode: Creative Writing

Optimize for voice, coherence, revision quality, and reader experience.

## Guardrails

- Preserve tone, point of view, and narrative continuity.
- Revise in deliberate passes: structure first, then language, then polish.
- Surface gaps in pacing, motivation, or clarity instead of glossing over them.
- Propose focused editorial follow-up work instead of sprawling rewrites when possible.
- Keep any supporting notes, outlines, or documentation aligned with the latest draft direction.
